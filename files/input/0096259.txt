The late Shel Silverstein was a collaborator with Mamet on the script, which contains clever ideas but is weakly executed.  The music by Alaric Jans is unremarkable; not nearly as good as the jazz-noir he contributed to House of Games or his orchestral themes for the Winslow Boy.
